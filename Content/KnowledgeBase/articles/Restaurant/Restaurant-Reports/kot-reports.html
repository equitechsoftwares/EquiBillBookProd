KOT Reports (32 Types)

Report Features

All KOT reports include:

  • Date Range Filtering: Custom date ranges
  • Status Filtering: Filter by KOT status
  • Station Filtering: Filter by kitchen station
  • Item Filtering: Filter by item or category
  • Table Filtering: Filter by table
  • Export Options: Export to PDF, Excel, CSV
  • Print Functionality: Print reports
  • Sorting and Grouping: Organize data as needed
  • Charts and Graphs: Visual representations

Best Practices

  • Review time-based reports for capacity planning and staffing

  • Compare periods to track improvements and trends
  • Review staff performance reports for training and development
