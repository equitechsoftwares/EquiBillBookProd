Report Features

All booking reports include:

  • Date Range Filtering: Custom date ranges
  • Status Filtering: Filter by booking status
  • Table Filtering: Filter by table or table type
  • Customer Filtering: Filter by customer
  • Export Options: Export to PDF, Excel, CSV
  • Print Functionality: Print reports
  • Sorting and Grouping: Organize data as needed

Best Practices

  • Analyze cancellation and no-show reports to improve policies
  • Use time-based reports for capacity planning and staffing
  • Compare periods to identify trends and improvements
