Report Features

Restaurant Performance Reports include:

  • Custom Date Ranges: Analyze any time period

  • Drill-down Capabilities: Click to view detailed data

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Key metrics tracked in performance reports:

  • Revenue Metrics: Total revenue, average revenue, revenue growth
  • Booking Metrics: Booking volume, conversion rates, no-shows
  • Utilization Metrics: Table occupancy, turnover rates
  • Efficiency Metrics: Preparation times, service times
  • Customer Metrics: Retention, frequency, satisfaction
  • Profitability Metrics: Profit margins, cost ratios
