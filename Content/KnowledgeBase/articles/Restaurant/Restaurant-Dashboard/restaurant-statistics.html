Restaurant Statistics

The Restaurant Statistics section in EquiBillBook's Restaurant Dashboard provides comprehensive analytics and insights about your restaurant operations, helping you make data-driven decisions. This guide explains how to access and use Restaurant Statistics.

Accessing Restaurant Statistics

To access Restaurant Statistics:

  1. Navigate to Restaurant â†’ Restaurant Dashboard
  2. Click on the Statistics or Analytics tab
  3. Select your branch and date range

Booking Statistics

Comprehensive booking analytics:

Booking Volume

  • Total Bookings: Total number of bookings for the selected period
  • Booking Trends: Booking volume over time (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • Peak Booking Times: Times when most bookings occur
  • Peak Booking Days: Days with highest booking volume

Booking Status Distribution

  • Percentage breakdown by status (Confirmed, Pending, Completed, Cancelled)
  • Booking conversion rates
  • Cancellation rates and trends
  • No-show rates

Customer Analytics

  • New vs Returning Customers: Customer retention metrics
  • Average Party Size: Average number of guests per booking
  • Customer Frequency: How often customers visit
  • Top Customers: Most frequent customers

Table Utilization Statistics

Metrics about table usage and efficiency:

  • Occupancy Rate: Percentage of time tables are occupied
  • Average Table Turnover: How many times tables are used per day
  • Table Performance: Revenue and utilization by table
  • Peak Utilization Times: When tables are most utilized
  • Table Type Performance: Performance by table type (VIP, Normal, Outdoor)

KOT Statistics

Kitchen Order Ticket analytics:

Order Volume

  • Total KOTs: Number of orders for the period
  • Average Orders per Day: Daily order averages
  • Peak Order Times: Busiest ordering periods

Order Performance

  • Average Preparation Time: Time taken to prepare orders
  • Order Status Distribution: Breakdown by status
  • Cancellation Rate: Percentage of cancelled orders
  • Station Performance: Performance by kitchen station

Item Analytics

  • Top Selling Items: Most popular menu items
  • Item Performance: Revenue and quantity by item
  • Category Performance: Performance by item category
  • Slow Moving Items: Items with low sales

Revenue Statistics

Financial performance metrics:

Revenue Overview

  • Total Revenue: Total revenue for the period
  • Daily Revenue: Revenue breakdown by day
  • Revenue Trends: Revenue growth or decline over time
  • Average Revenue per Booking: Average spending per booking
  • Average Revenue per Table: Average revenue generated per table

Revenue by Source

  • Revenue from bookings
  • Revenue from walk-in customers
  • Revenue by payment method
  • Revenue by table type

Time-based Revenue

  • Hourly Revenue: Revenue by hour of the day
  • Day of Week Revenue: Revenue by day
  • Monthly Revenue: Revenue trends by month

Performance Metrics

Key performance indicators:

  • Table Turnover Rate: Efficiency metric
  • Average Dining Duration: Time customers spend at tables
  • Kitchen Efficiency: Preparation time metrics
  • Staff Performance: Performance by waiter/staff member
  • Customer Satisfaction Indicators: Metrics related to service quality

Comparative Analysis

Compare performance across different periods:

  • Day-over-Day Comparison: Compare today with previous days
  • Week-over-Week: Compare current week with previous weeks
  • Month-over-Month: Monthly comparisons
  • Year-over-Year: Annual comparisons
  • Period Comparisons: Custom date range comparisons

Multi-branch Statistics

If you have multiple branches:

  • Compare performance across branches
  • Branch-specific statistics
  • Aggregate statistics for all branches
  • Identify best-performing branches

Date Range Selection

Customize the period for statistics:

  • Today: Statistics for today only
  • This Week: Current week's data
  • This Month: Current month's data
  • This Year: Year-to-date data
  • Custom Range: Select specific start and end dates
  • Last Period: Compare with previous period

Charts and Visualizations

Statistics are presented in various formats:

  • Line Charts: Trends over time
  • Bar Charts: Comparisons between categories
  • Pie Charts: Distribution percentages
  • Tables: Detailed numerical data
  • Heatmaps: Time-based patterns

Exporting Statistics

Export statistics for further analysis:

  • Export to PDF: Generate PDF reports
  • Export to Excel: Export data for analysis
  • Print Statistics: Print reports
  • Share Reports: Share with team members

Drill-down Capabilities

Click on statistics to view detailed information:

  • View underlying data
  • Access related reports
  • Filter and analyze specific segments
  • Export detailed data

  • Use statistics to make staffing and operational decisions
  • Share relevant statistics with staff for performance improvement
  • Set up regular review schedules
