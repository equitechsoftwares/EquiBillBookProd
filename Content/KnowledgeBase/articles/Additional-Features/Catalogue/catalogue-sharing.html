Catalogue Sharing

Sharing your public catalogue allows customers to discover and browse your products. This guide covers various methods to share your catalogue and reach your target audience.

Getting Your Catalogue URL

To share your catalogue, you need the public URL:

  1. Open your catalogue
  2. Find the Public URL or Catalogue Link
  3. Copy the URL (format: https://yoursite.com/c/[slug])
  4. Use this URL for sharing

Sharing Methods

Multiple ways to share your catalogue:

1. Direct Link Sharing

Share the URL directly:

  • Copy URL: Copy the catalogue URL
  • Paste and Share: Paste in messages, emails, or documents
  • Simple Method: Easiest way to share
  • Universal: Works across all platforms

2. Email Sharing

Share via email:

  • Email Marketing: Include link in email campaigns
  • Customer Emails: Send to existing customers
  • Newsletter: Add to newsletter
  • Personalized Emails: Send personalized catalogue links
  • Email Signature: Add link to email signature

3. Social Media Sharing

Share on social platforms:

  • Facebook: Post catalogue link on Facebook
  • Instagram: Add link in bio or stories
  • Twitter/X: Tweet the catalogue link
  • LinkedIn: Share on LinkedIn for B2B
  • WhatsApp: Share link in WhatsApp messages or groups
  • Other Platforms: Share on any social media platform

4. Website Integration

Add catalogue to your website:

  • Navigation Menu: Add "Catalogue" or "Products" link
  • Homepage Button: Add prominent button on homepage
  • Product Page: Link from product pages
  • Footer Link: Add link in website footer
  • Sidebar Widget: Add catalogue widget (if available)

5. QR Code Sharing

If QR code is available:

  • Generate QR Code: Create QR code for catalogue URL
  • Print Materials: Add QR code to business cards, flyers, posters
  • In-Store Display: Display QR code in physical store
  • Packaging: Add QR code to product packaging
  • Easy Access: Customers scan to access catalogue

6. WhatsApp Sharing

Share via WhatsApp:

  • Direct Messages: Send link to individual contacts
  • WhatsApp Groups: Share in relevant groups
  • WhatsApp Business: Use WhatsApp Business for professional sharing
  • Broadcast Lists: Send to multiple contacts
  • Status Updates: Share in WhatsApp status

7. SMS Sharing

Share via text message:

  • Customer Database: Send to customer phone numbers
  • SMS Campaigns: Include in SMS marketing campaigns
  • Short Links: Use URL shortener for SMS (if needed)

Sharing Best Practices

Tips for effective sharing:

  • Clear Call-to-Action: Use compelling text when sharing
  • Context: Provide context about what customers will find
  • Timing: Share at appropriate times for your audience
  • Frequency: Don't over-share, but keep catalogue visible
  • Targeting: Share with relevant audience
  • Follow-up: Follow up with customers who show interest

Password-Protected Catalogues

If catalogue requires password:

  • Share Password: Provide password along with URL
  • Secure Sharing: Share password through secure channels
  • Access Control: Control who can access the catalogue
  • Password Updates: Update password if needed

Tracking Shared Links

Monitor how your catalogue is accessed:

  • Google Analytics: Track visits if Analytics is configured
  • Referral Sources: See where visitors come from
  • Popular Items: Identify most viewed products
  • Customer Engagement: Track interactions and enquiries

Multiple Catalogue Sharing

If you have multiple catalogues:

  • Targeted Sharing: Share specific catalogues to specific audiences
  • Seasonal Catalogues: Share seasonal catalogues at appropriate times
  • Customer Segments: Share different catalogues to different customer groups
  • Organized Sharing: Keep track of which catalogue is shared where

Embedding on Website

To embed catalogue on your website:

  • Direct Link: Add link button to your website
  • Iframe: Embed catalogue in iframe (if supported)
  • Widget: Use catalogue widget (if available)
  • Seamless Integration: Make catalogue part of your website

Sharing Checklist

Before sharing, ensure:

  • Catalogue is published and active
  • All items are correctly displayed
  • Pricing information is accurate
  • Images are loading properly
  • Business information is complete
  • Interactive features are working
  • Mobile view is optimized
  • URL is correct and accessible

Best Practices

  • Share catalogue URL through multiple channels
  • Use clear and compelling messaging when sharing
  • Target the right audience for your catalogue
  • Monitor how customers access your catalogue
  • Respond promptly to customer enquiries
  • Update catalogue content regularly
  • Promote new catalogues when published
  • Use social media effectively for sharing
  • Include catalogue link in all marketing materials

Next Steps

After sharing your catalogue:

  • Monitor customer access and engagement
  • Track enquiries and interactions
  • Gather customer feedback
  • Update catalogue based on feedback
  • Continue promoting through various channels
