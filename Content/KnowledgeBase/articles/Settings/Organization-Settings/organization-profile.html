Organization Profile

Your organization profile contains the core information about your business that identifies your company across all EquiBillBook documents and reports. This is the foundation of your business identity in the system.

What is Organization Profile?

The organization profile includes essential business identification details such as:

  • Business Name: Your official registered business or company name
  • Business Type: The legal structure of your business (Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, LLC, Corporation, etc.)
  • Industry Type: The industry category your business operates in
  • Business Start Date: The date when your business officially started operations
  • Country: The country where your business is registered
  • Website URL: Your business website address (optional)

Accessing Organization Profile

To view or edit your organization profile:

  1. Navigate to Settings → Organization Settings → Business Settings
  2. You'll see the "Organization Profile" or "Business Information" section at the top
  3. This section contains all your core business identification details

Key Profile Components

Business Name

Your business name is the most critical piece of information:

  • Appears on all invoices, quotations, and business documents
  • Should match your legal business registration exactly
  • Cannot be changed easily after setup (requires admin access or support)
  • Used for legal compliance and tax reporting

Business Type

Select the legal structure that best describes your business:

  • Sole Proprietorship: Single owner business
  • Partnership: Business owned by two or more partners
  • LLC (Limited Liability Company): Limited liability structure
  • Corporation: Incorporated business entity
  • Other: Other legal structures as applicable

Industry Type

Choose the industry category that best matches your business:

  • Retail
  • Wholesale
  • Manufacturing
  • Service
  • Restaurant/Food Service
  • Healthcare
  • Technology
  • And many more...

Note: Selecting the correct industry helps EquiBillBook provide relevant default settings and features for your business type.

Business Start Date

The business start date is important for:

  • Financial year calculations
  • Historical reporting
  • Tax compliance
  • Business age calculations

Important: This date cannot be changed after transactions are recorded, so ensure it's accurate.

Country Selection

Your country selection affects:

  • Currency settings and default currency
  • Tax configuration options (GST, VAT, Sales Tax, etc.)
  • Regional formatting (date format, number format, time zone)
  • Legal compliance requirements
  • Available payment gateways

Step-by-Step Profile Setup

Step 1: Enter Business Name

Enter your official business name exactly as it appears on your business registration documents. This name will be used on all official documents.

Step 2: Select Business Type

Choose the legal structure that matches your business registration. If you're unsure, consult with your accountant or legal advisor.

Step 3: Choose Industry Type

Select the industry that best describes your business. You can select multiple types if your business spans multiple industries.

Step 4: Set Business Start Date

Enter the date when your business officially started operations. This should match your business registration date.

Step 5: Select Country

Choose your country from the dropdown list. This will set default regional settings for your account.

Step 6: Add Website (Optional)

If you have a business website, enter the URL. This may appear on invoices and other documents.

Step 7: Save Your Profile

Review all information carefully, then click "Update" or "Save" to save your organization profile.

Where Profile Information Appears

Your organization profile information is displayed on:

  • All Invoices: Business name in header
  • Quotations: Business identification
  • Purchase Bills: Your business details
  • Reports: Report headers and footers
  • Email Communications: Business identification
  • Legal Documents: All official business documents

Updating Your Profile

To update your organization profile:

  1. Navigate to Organization Settings → Business Settings
  2. Modify the fields you want to update
  3. Note: Business name changes may require admin approval or support assistance
  4. Save your changes

Important: Some fields like business name and start date may have restrictions on changes to maintain data integrity and legal compliance.

Best Practices

  • Accuracy First: Ensure all information matches your legal business registration documents
  • Complete Information: Fill in all relevant fields for better system functionality
  • Regular Review: Periodically review your profile to ensure it's up-to-date
  • Legal Compliance: Ensure your business name matches official registration
  • Industry Selection: Choose the most accurate industry type for better feature alignment

Troubleshooting

Business name field is disabled?

  • Business name can only be changed by administrators or through support
  • This is to prevent accidental changes that could affect legal documents
  • Contact EquiBillBook support if you need to change your business name

Cannot change business start date?

  • Start date is locked after transactions are recorded to maintain data integrity
  • Contact support if you need to correct this date
  • You may need to provide documentation for the change

Industry type options not showing my business type?

  • Select the closest matching industry type
  • You can select multiple types if applicable
  • Contact support if you need additional industry types added

Related Settings

After setting up your organization profile, you should also configure:

  • Business Details: Additional business information
  • Logo and Branding: Upload your business logo
  • Address Management: Set up your business address
  • Contact Information: Add contact details
  • GST/Tax Registration: Configure tax details
  • Financial Year Settings: Set up your financial year

Your organization profile is the foundation of your business identity in EquiBillBook. Take time to ensure all information is accurate and complete!
