Logo and Branding in Templates

Adding your logo and branding to invoice templates in EquiBillBook helps create professional, branded documents that represent your business. This guide covers how to add and configure logos and branding elements in your templates.

Why Add Logo and Branding?

Logo and branding help:

  • Create professional appearance
  • Build brand recognition
  • Establish credibility
  • Differentiate from competitors
  • Reinforce brand identity

Accessing Logo and Branding Settings

To configure logo and branding:

  1. Navigate to Settings â†’ Invoice Template Settings
  2. Select a template
  3. Click "Customize" or "Edit"
  4. Go to "Logo and Branding" section

Adding Your Logo

Step 1: Prepare Your Logo

Before uploading, ensure your logo:

  • Format: PNG (recommended) or JPG
  • Size: High resolution (at least 300x300 pixels)
  • Background: Transparent background (PNG) or white background
  • File Size: Under 2MB (recommended)
  • Quality: Clear and sharp

Step 2: Upload Logo

  1. Click "Upload Logo" or "Choose File"
  2. Select your logo file
  3. Wait for upload to complete
  4. Logo will appear in preview

Step 3: Configure Logo Settings

After uploading, configure:

Logo Position
  • Left: Logo on the left side
  • Center: Logo centered
  • Right: Logo on the right side
Logo Size
  • Small: Compact logo
  • Medium: Standard size
  • Large: Prominent logo
  • Custom: Set specific dimensions
Logo Alignment
  • Align with header
  • Align with company details
  • Independent positioning

Branding Elements

1. Color Scheme

Apply your brand colors:

Primary Color
  • Your main brand color
  • Used in headers, highlights
  • Sets overall theme
Secondary Color
  • Accent color
  • Used for emphasis
  • Complements primary color
Text Colors
  • Header text color
  • Body text color
  • Ensure good contrast for readability

2. Company Information

Display your business details:

  • Company Name: Your business name
  • Tagline: Business tagline (optional)
  • Address: Business address
  • Contact: Phone, email, website
  • Registration: Business registration numbers

3. Typography

Use fonts that match your brand:

  • Select professional fonts
  • Ensure readability
  • Maintain consistency
  • Use appropriate font sizes

Logo Best Practices

  • High Quality: Use high-resolution logo
  • Appropriate Size: Not too large or too small
  • Good Position: Place where it's visible but not intrusive
  • Consistent: Use same logo across all templates
  • Professional: Ensure logo looks professional

Branding Best Practices

  • Consistency: Use consistent branding across templates
  • Color Harmony: Ensure colors work well together
  • Readability: Maintain text readability
  • Balance: Balance branding with information
  • Professional: Keep design professional

Multiple Brand Support

If you manage multiple brands:

  • Create separate templates for each brand
  • Use different logos per brand
  • Apply brand-specific colors
  • Customize company details per brand

Troubleshooting

Logo not displaying?

  • Check file format (PNG or JPG)
  • Verify file size is within limits
  • Check if upload completed successfully
  • Verify logo position settings
  • Clear browser cache

Logo appears blurry?

  • Use higher resolution logo
  • Check logo size settings
  • Verify original file quality
  • Use PNG format for better quality

Colors not matching brand?

  • Verify color codes (hex values)
  • Check color format
  • Use color picker for accuracy
  • Preview colors before saving

Logo too large/small?

  • Adjust logo size settings
  • Resize logo file if needed
  • Use custom dimensions
  • Preview and adjust

Logo File Requirements

  • Formats: PNG (recommended), JPG
  • Size: Maximum 2MB file size
  • Dimensions: At least 300x300 pixels
  • Background: Transparent (PNG) or white
  • Aspect Ratio: Square or rectangular
