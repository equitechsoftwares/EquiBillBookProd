POS Settings

POS (Point of Sale) Settings in EquiBillBook allow you to configure your retail point-of-sale operations, including screen layout, receipt printing, payment methods, and cash register management. This guide covers all POS-related settings.

Accessing POS Settings

To configure POS settings:

  1. Navigate to Settings → Sales Settings → POS Settings
  2. Configure POS screen, receipt, and payment options
  3. Click "Update" or "Save" to apply changes

POS Module Activation

First, ensure POS module is enabled:

  • Enable POS: Activate POS functionality
  • POS Access: Control who can access POS
  • POS Locations: Configure POS for specific locations/branches

POS Screen Configuration

Configure the POS interface layout and display:

Screen Layout

  • Item Display: Configure how items are displayed (grid, list, tiles)
  • Item Images: Show or hide product images
  • Item Search: Enable search functionality
  • Category Display: Show item categories
  • Quick Items: Configure frequently sold items

Cart Display

  • Cart Position: Set cart position (right, left, bottom)
  • Item Details: Show item details in cart
  • Quantity Controls: Configure quantity adjustment buttons
  • Subtotal Display: Show subtotals and totals

Receipt Printing Settings

Configure receipt printing options:

Print Options

  • Auto Print Receipt: Automatically print after sale
  • Print on Payment: Print when payment is received
  • Print Duplicate: Allow printing duplicate receipts
  • Receipt Printer: Select default receipt printer

Receipt Format

  • Receipt Template: Select receipt template
  • Receipt Size: Configure receipt paper size
  • Show Logo: Display business logo on receipt
  • Show Address: Display business address
  • Show Tax Details: Display tax breakdown
  • Show Payment Method: Display payment method used

Payment Methods Configuration

Configure accepted payment methods in POS:

Payment Options

  • Cash: Enable cash payments
  • Card: Enable card payments (credit/debit)
  • Digital Wallet: Enable digital wallet payments
  • UPI: Enable UPI payments
  • Cheque: Enable cheque payments
  • Credit: Enable credit sales

Payment Processing

  • Payment Gateway: Configure payment gateway integration
  • Change Calculation: Auto-calculate change amount
  • Round-off: Round payment amounts
  • Partial Payment: Allow partial payments

Cash Register Management

Configure cash register and drawer settings:

Cash Drawer

  • Open Drawer: Configure when cash drawer opens
  • Drawer Amount: Set opening cash amount
  • Cash Count: Enable cash counting at shift end
  • Cash Reconciliation: Enable cash reconciliation

Shift Management

  • Shift Start/End: Track shift timings
  • Opening Balance: Set opening cash balance
  • Closing Balance: Record closing cash balance
  • Shift Reports: Generate shift-wise reports

Barcode Scanning

Configure barcode scanning functionality:

  • Enable Barcode: Enable barcode scanning
  • Barcode Scanner: Configure barcode scanner device
  • Auto Add Item: Automatically add item on scan
  • Barcode Search: Search items by barcode

Discount and Pricing

Configure discount and pricing options:

  • Allow Discount: Enable discount application
  • Discount Limit: Set maximum discount percentage
  • Price Override: Allow price modification
  • Customer Pricing: Apply customer-specific prices

Customer Selection

Configure customer selection in POS:

  • Customer Required: Make customer selection mandatory
  • Walk-in Customer: Enable walk-in customer option
  • Customer Search: Enable customer search
  • Customer Display: Show customer information

Tax Configuration

Configure tax settings for POS:

  • Tax Calculation: Configure tax calculation method
  • Tax Display: Show tax on receipt
  • Tax Inclusive: Prices include tax
  • Tax Exempt: Allow tax-exempt sales

Security Settings

Configure POS security and access control:

  • User Authentication: Require login for POS access
  • Permission Control: Control POS permissions
  • Cancel Permission: Control who can cancel sales
  • Refund Permission: Control who can process refunds

Best Practices

  • Receipt Printing: Always print receipts for customer records
  • Cash Management: Regularly reconcile cash drawer
  • Security: Use proper authentication and permissions
  • Training: Train staff on POS operations
  • Backup: Regular backup of POS data

Common Scenarios

Retail Store

  • Enable barcode scanning
  • Auto print receipts
  • Multiple payment methods
  • Cash drawer management

Restaurant/Cafe

  • Table-based sales
  • Order management
  • Split bills
  • Tips handling
