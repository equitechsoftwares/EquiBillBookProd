Payment Methods Setup

Payment methods setup in EquiBillBook allows you to configure which payment methods customers can use to make payments. This guide explains how to set up and manage payment methods effectively.

What are Payment Methods?

Payment methods are the various ways customers can pay for invoices, bookings, or purchases. EquiBillBook supports multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, digital wallets, and bank transfers. You can enable or disable specific payment methods based on your business needs.

Accessing Payment Methods Setup

To access payment methods setup:

  1. Navigate to Settings → Online Payment Settings → Payment Methods Setup
  2. View available payment methods
  3. Enable or disable payment methods
  4. Configure method-specific settings

Credit Card Payments

Configure credit card payments:

  • Enable Credit Cards: Turn on credit card payments
  • Supported Cards: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, RuPay, Discover
  • Card Verification: Enable CVV and address verification
  • 3D Secure: Enable 3D Secure authentication
  • Card Storage: Allow customers to save cards (if supported)
  • International Cards: Accept international credit cards

Debit Card Payments

Configure debit card payments:

  • Enable Debit Cards: Turn on debit card payments
  • Supported Cards: Visa Debit, Mastercard Debit, RuPay Debit
  • Card Verification: Enable CVV verification
  • PIN Verification: Require PIN for debit card payments
  • Domestic Cards: Accept domestic debit cards

UPI Payments

Configure UPI (Unified Payments Interface) payments:

  • Enable UPI: Turn on UPI payments
  • UPI Apps: Support popular UPI apps (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, etc.)
  • UPI ID: Configure UPI merchant ID
  • QR Code: Generate UPI QR codes for payments
  • UPI Intent: Support UPI intent URLs

Net Banking

Configure net banking payments:

  • Enable Net Banking: Turn on net banking option
  • Supported Banks: Select banks to support
  • Bank List: Display list of supported banks
  • Bank Logos: Show bank logos for easy selection
  • Instant Transfer: Support instant bank transfers (if available)

Digital Wallets

Configure digital wallet payments:

  • Enable Wallets: Turn on wallet payments
  • Supported Wallets: Paytm, PhonePe, Amazon Pay, etc.
  • Wallet Selection: Allow customers to choose wallet
  • Wallet Balance: Check wallet balance (if supported)
  • Wallet Top-up: Support wallet top-up payments

Bank Transfer

Configure bank transfer payments:

  • Enable Bank Transfer: Turn on bank transfer option
  • Bank Details: Display bank account details
  • Manual Verification: Manually verify bank transfers
  • Transfer Instructions: Provide transfer instructions to customers
  • Reference Number: Require reference number for verification

EMI (Equated Monthly Installments)

Configure EMI payment options:

  • Enable EMI: Turn on EMI payment option
  • EMI Tenure: Configure available EMI tenures (3, 6, 9, 12 months)
  • Minimum Amount: Set minimum amount for EMI
  • Interest Rates: Configure EMI interest rates
  • EMI Partners: Select EMI partner banks/NBFCs

Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL)

Configure BNPL payment options:

  • Enable BNPL: Turn on BNPL option
  • BNPL Providers: Integrate BNPL providers
  • Credit Limits: Configure credit limits
  • Repayment Terms: Set repayment terms
  • Eligibility Check: Check customer eligibility

Cash on Delivery (COD)

Configure cash on delivery (if applicable):

  • Enable COD: Turn on cash on delivery option
  • COD Charges: Configure COD charges
  • COD Limits: Set maximum amount for COD
  • Service Areas: Define areas where COD is available
  • COD Verification: Verify COD payments

Payment Method Configuration

Configure general payment method settings:

  • Default Method: Set default payment method
  • Method Order: Arrange payment methods in preferred order
  • Method Icons: Display icons for payment methods
  • Method Descriptions: Add descriptions for payment methods
  • Method Availability: Set availability by location/region

Payment Method Restrictions

Configure restrictions for payment methods:

  • Minimum Amount: Set minimum amount per method
  • Maximum Amount: Set maximum amount per method
  • Time Restrictions: Restrict methods by time (business hours)
  • Customer Restrictions: Restrict methods for specific customers
  • Location Restrictions: Restrict methods by location

Payment Method Fees

Configure fees for payment methods:

  • Transaction Fees: Set fees per payment method
  • Fee Structure: Percentage or fixed fee
  • Fee Display: Show fees to customers
  • Fee Calculation: Automatic fee calculation
  • Fee Waiver: Waive fees for specific conditions

Payment Method Testing

Test payment methods:

  1. Enable test mode
  2. Test each enabled payment method
  3. Verify payment processing works
  4. Check payment recording
  5. Test error scenarios
  6. Verify notifications

Payment Method Analytics

Monitor payment method usage:

  • Usage Statistics: Track usage of each method
  • Success Rates: Monitor success rates per method
  • Revenue by Method: Track revenue per payment method
  • Customer Preferences: Analyze customer payment preferences
  • Method Performance: Compare method performance

Payment Method Best Practices

  • Offer Multiple Options: Provide various payment methods for customer convenience
  • Prioritize Popular Methods: Enable most popular methods in your region
  • Test Regularly: Test all enabled payment methods regularly
  • Monitor Usage: Monitor which methods customers prefer
  • Optimize Order: Arrange methods in order of popularity
  • Clear Descriptions: Provide clear descriptions for each method
  • Handle Errors: Provide clear error messages for failed payments

Troubleshooting Payment Methods

Common issues and solutions:

  • Method Not Showing: Verify method is enabled
  • Payment Failing: Check gateway configuration
  • Method Not Working: Verify gateway supports the method
  • Fees Not Calculating: Check fee configuration
  • Restrictions Not Working: Verify restriction settings

Properly configured payment methods provide customers with convenient payment options, improving payment collection and customer satisfaction. Enable methods relevant to your business and customer base, test thoroughly, and monitor usage regularly.
