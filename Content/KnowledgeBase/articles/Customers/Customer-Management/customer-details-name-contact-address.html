Customer Details (Name, Contact, Address)

Accurate customer details are crucial for effective communication, delivery, and record-keeping. This guide explains how to manage customer basic information including name, contact details, and addresses in EquiBillBook.

Customer Name

The customer name is a required field and should be entered as follows:

  • Individual Customers: Enter full name (e.g., "John Smith" or "Priya Sharma")
  • Business Customers: Enter the business name or proprietor name
  • Best Practice: Use consistent naming format across all customers
  • The name appears on invoices, statements, and reports

Contact Information

Maintain accurate contact details for effective communication:

Primary Mobile Number

  • Required Field: Primary mobile number is mandatory
  • Format: Enter 10-digit mobile number (or as per your country's format)
  • Uniqueness: System may check for duplicate mobile numbers
  • Used for SMS notifications, WhatsApp communications, and contact purposes

Email Address

  • Optional Field: Email is not mandatory but recommended
  • Format: Enter valid email address format (e.g., customer@example.com)
  • Used for sending invoices, quotations, payment reminders, and notifications
  • Email receipts and documents can be sent directly to this address

Alternative Mobile Number

  • Optional Field: Add an alternate contact number
  • Useful when the primary number is not reachable
  • Helps in maintaining communication backup

Business Information

For business customers, you can add:

  • Business Name: Enter the registered business name or trade name
  • Business Legal Name: Enter the legal entity name (if different from trade name)
  • Business Registration Number: Enter registration number if applicable
  • This information appears on invoices and legal documents

Address Management

Managing addresses correctly ensures accurate deliveries and tax calculations:

Billing Address

The billing address is the primary address for the customer:

  • Street Address: Enter building number, street name, area
  • City: Select or enter the city
  • State: Select the state or province
  • Country: Select the country
  • PIN/Postal Code: Enter the postal or PIN code
  • This address appears on invoices and is used for billing purposes

Shipping Address

If the shipping address differs from the billing address:

  • Enable the option: "Shipping address is different from billing address"
  • Enter a separate shipping address with all details
  • Useful for businesses that ship to different locations
  • Shipping address is used for delivery challans and shipment tracking

Date Fields

Additional date information you can track:

  • Date of Birth (DOB): Enter customer's birth date (useful for personal customers, birthday campaigns)
  • Joining Date: Enter when the customer was acquired or joined (useful for customer lifecycle analysis)

Importance of Accurate Details

Accurate customer details ensure:

  • Effective Communication: Reach customers for invoices, reminders, and updates
  • Accurate Deliveries: Correct shipping addresses ensure timely deliveries
  • Tax Compliance: Proper addresses help in accurate tax calculations (GST, place of supply)
  • Legal Compliance: Complete information required for invoices and legal documents
  • Customer Service: Quick access to contact information for customer support
  • Marketing: Email and mobile numbers enable marketing campaigns

Data Validation

The system validates:

  • Mobile number format and length
  • Email address format
  • Required fields are filled
  • Address completeness

Best Practices

  • Complete Information: Fill in all available fields for better customer management
  • Verify Contact Details: Verify mobile numbers and email addresses before saving
  • Update Regularly: Keep contact information and addresses up-to-date
  • Consistent Format: Use consistent naming and address formatting
  • Separate Addresses: Use shipping address when deliveries go to different locations
  • Privacy: Handle customer contact information securely and in compliance with privacy regulations

Troubleshooting

Cannot Save Customer: Check that required fields (Name and Mobile) are filled, verify mobile number format, and ensure email format is correct if provided.

Duplicate Mobile Number: The system may prevent duplicate mobile numbers. Use the existing customer record or contact support if this is a legitimate duplicate.

Address Not Saving: Ensure all address fields are properly filled, select valid city/state/country from dropdowns, and verify PIN code format.
