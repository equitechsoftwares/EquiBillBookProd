Customer Sales Reports

Customer sales reports provide detailed analysis of sales transactions by customer in EquiBillBook. This guide will help you generate, analyze, and use customer sales reports to understand customer buying patterns, identify top customers, and make informed business decisions.

What is a Customer Sales Report?

A customer sales report shows:

  • Sales transactions by customer
  • Total sales amount per customer
  • Number of invoices per customer
  • Sales trends over time
  • Item-wise sales by customer
  • Sales by location or branch
  • Profit margins by customer

Accessing Customer Sales Reports

To generate customer sales reports:

  1. Navigate to Reports from the main menu
  2. Click on Sales Reports or Customer Reports
  3. Select "Sales by Customer", "Customer Sales Report", or "Customer-wise Sales"

Alternatively, access from:

  • Customers → Select customer → "Sales History" or "Sales Report"
  • Sales → Sales Reports → "By Customer"

Report Configuration Options

Customize customer sales reports with various options:

1. Date Range

  • From Date: Start date of the reporting period
  • To Date: End date of the reporting period
  • Predefined Periods: Today, this week, this month, this quarter, this year, last year
  • Custom Range: Select any date range

2. Customer Selection

  • All Customers: Include sales for all customers
  • Specific Customer: Filter by individual customer
  • Customer Group: Filter by customer group or category
  • Top Customers: Show top N customers by sales
  • By Location: Filter by branch or location

3. Sales Type

  • All Sales: Include all sales transactions
  • Invoices Only: Sales invoices only
  • Returns Only: Sales returns only
  • Net Sales: Sales minus returns

4. Report Format

  • Summary Report: Total sales per customer
  • Detailed Report: All invoices with line items
  • Item-wise Report: Sales by item for each customer
  • Comparative Report: Compare sales across periods

Understanding Customer Sales Reports

Sales reports display information such as:

  • Customer Name: Name of the customer
  • Customer Code: Customer identifier
  • Total Sales: Total sales amount for the period
  • Number of Invoices: Count of sales invoices
  • Average Invoice Value: Average amount per invoice
  • Largest Invoice: Highest single invoice amount
  • Last Sale Date: Date of most recent sale
  • Sales Growth: Percentage change from previous period

Report Summary

The report includes summary statistics:

  • Total Customers: Number of customers with sales
  • Total Sales: Sum of all customer sales
  • Average Sales per Customer: Average sales amount
  • Top Customer: Customer with highest sales
  • Sales Distribution: Distribution of sales across customers
  • Growth Rate: Overall sales growth percentage
