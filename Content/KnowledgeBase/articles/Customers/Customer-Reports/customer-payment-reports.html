Customer Payment Reports

Customer payment reports provide detailed analysis of all payments received from customers in EquiBillBook. This guide will help you generate, analyze, and use payment reports to track collections, monitor payment trends, and manage accounts receivable effectively.

What is a Customer Payment Report?

A customer payment report shows:

  • All payments received from customers
  • Payment dates and amounts
  • Payment methods used
  • Invoices paid against each payment
  • Payment accounts and references
  • Payment status and reconciliation
  • Payment trends and analysis

Accessing Customer Payment Reports

To generate customer payment reports:

  1. Navigate to Reports from the main menu
  2. Click on Customer Payment Reports or Receivables Reports
  3. Select "Customer Payment Report" or "Payment History Report"

Alternatively, access from:

  • Customers → Select customer → "Payment History"
  • Accounts → Payment Accounts → View transactions
  • Banking → Bank Deposits (for bank payments)

Report Configuration Options

Customize payment reports with various options:

1. Date Range

  • From Date: Start date of the reporting period
  • To Date: End date of the reporting period
  • Predefined Periods: Today, this week, this month, this quarter, this year
  • Custom Range: Select any date range

2. Customer Selection

  • All Customers: Include payments from all customers
  • Specific Customer: Filter by individual customer
  • Customer Group: Filter by customer group or category
  • By Location: Filter by branch or location

3. Payment Method

  • All Methods: Include all payment methods
  • Cash: Cash payments only
  • Cheque: Cheque payments only
  • Bank Transfer: Bank transfer payments
  • Credit Card: Credit card payments
  • Online Payment: Online payment gateway payments
  • Other Methods: Other payment methods

4. Payment Account

  • All Accounts: Include payments to all accounts
  • Cash Account: Cash account payments only
  • Bank Account: Specific bank account payments
  • Multiple Accounts: Select multiple payment accounts

5. Payment Status

  • All Statuses: Include all payment statuses
  • Cleared: Cleared payments only
  • Pending: Pending payments only
  • Bounced: Bounced or failed payments

Understanding Payment Reports

Payment reports display information such as:

  • Payment Date: Date when payment was received
  • Customer Name: Customer who made the payment
  • Payment Amount: Total payment amount
  • Payment Method: How payment was made
  • Reference Number: Cheque number, transaction ID, receipt number
  • Account: Account where payment was deposited
  • Invoices Paid: List of invoices paid with this payment
  • Status: Payment status (Cleared, Pending, Bounced)

Payment Report Summary

The report includes summary statistics:

  • Total Payments: Total number of payments
  • Total Amount: Sum of all payment amounts
  • By Payment Method: Breakdown by payment method
  • By Account: Breakdown by payment account
  • By Customer: Breakdown by customer
  • Average Payment: Average payment amount
  • Largest Payment: Highest single payment
