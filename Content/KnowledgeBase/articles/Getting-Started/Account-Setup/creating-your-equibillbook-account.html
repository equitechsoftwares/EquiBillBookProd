Creating Your EquiBillBook Account

Welcome to EquiBillBook! Creating your account is quick and easy. Follow these simple steps to get started with your billing and accounting software.

Step 1: Visit the Registration Page

Navigate to the EquiBillBook registration page by clicking the "Register" or "Sign Up" button on the homepage, or visit /register directly.

Step 2: Fill in Your Details

You'll need to provide the following information:

  • Business Name: Enter your business or company name
  • Email Address: Use a valid email address that you have access to (this will be used for account verification and important notifications)
  • Phone Number: Enter your contact number
  • Password: Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters, including letters and numbers)
  • Confirm Password: Re-enter your password to confirm

Step 3: Choose Your Plan

Select the subscription plan that best fits your business needs. Don't worry - you can always upgrade or modify your plan later.

Step 4: Accept Terms and Conditions

Read and accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy to proceed with the registration.

Step 5: Submit Registration

Click the "Create Account" or "Register" button to submit your registration.

What Happens Next?

After submitting your registration:

  1. You'll receive a confirmation email at the address you provided
  2. Click the verification link in the email to verify your account
  3. Once verified, you can log in to your EquiBillBook account
  4. Complete your organization profile setup to start using the software

Tips for Account Creation

  • Use a business email address for better organization
  • Choose a strong, unique password that you don't use elsewhere
  • Keep your login credentials secure and don't share them with unauthorized users
  • If you don't receive the verification email, check your spam/junk folder

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during registration:

  • Check that all required fields are filled correctly
  • Ensure your email address is valid and accessible
  • Verify that your password meets the minimum requirements
  • Contact our support team if you continue to experience problems

Once your account is created and verified, you're ready to start managing your business with EquiBillBook!
