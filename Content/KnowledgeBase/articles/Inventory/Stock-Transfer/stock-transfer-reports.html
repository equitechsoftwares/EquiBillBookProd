Stock Transfer Reports

Stock transfer reports track all inventory transfers between locations, helping you monitor stock movement, optimize inventory distribution, and maintain accurate records across multiple locations. This guide explains how to use stock transfer reports in EquiBillBook.

What are Stock Transfer Reports?

Stock transfer reports show:

  • All stock transfers between locations
  • Transfer dates and quantities
  • Source and destination locations
  • Transfer status and workflow
  • Items transferred
  • Transfer approval status
  • Transfer patterns and trends

Why Stock Transfer Reports are Important

Stock transfer reports help you:

  • Track inter-location stock movements
  • Monitor transfer efficiency
  • Optimize inventory distribution
  • Identify transfer patterns
  • Ensure proper authorization
  • Maintain audit trail
  • Balance stock across locations

Accessing Stock Transfer Reports

To view stock transfer reports:

  1. Navigate to Reports → Inventory Reports
  2. Select Stock Transfer Report
  3. Alternatively, go to Items → Stock Reports → Stock Transfers

Types of Stock Transfers

Stock transfers include:

  • Inter-Branch Transfers: Between different branches
  • Inter-Location Transfers: Between different locations
  • Warehouse Transfers: Between warehouses
  • Store Transfers: Between retail stores
  • Return Transfers: Returning stock to source

Stock Transfer Report Information

The report typically displays:

  • Transfer Date: Date of transfer
  • Transfer Number: Reference number
  • Item Code: Item identifier
  • Item Name: Product name
  • Source Location: Location transferring stock
  • Destination Location: Location receiving stock
  • Quantity: Transferred quantity
  • Unit: Unit of measurement
  • Cost Price: Cost per unit
  • Total Value: Transfer value
  • Transfer Status: Pending, In Transit, Completed, Cancelled
  • Created By: User who created transfer
  • Approved By: User who approved transfer
  • Received By: User who received transfer
  • Notes: Transfer notes or remarks

Filtering Stock Transfer Reports

You can filter reports by:

  • Date Range: Specific period for analysis
  • Source Location: Filter by source location
  • Destination Location: Filter by destination location
  • Item: View transfers for specific items
  • Item Category: Filter by categories
  • Transfer Status: Filter by status
  • Created By: Filter by user
  • Transfer Type: Filter by transfer type

Transfer Status Workflow

Track transfer status:

  • Pending: Transfer created, awaiting approval
  • Approved: Transfer approved, ready for dispatch
  • In Transit: Stock in transit between locations
  • Completed: Transfer received and completed
  • Cancelled: Transfer cancelled
  • Rejected: Transfer rejected

Transfer vs Purchase

Decide between transfer and purchase:

  • Purchase if stock is needed system-wide
