Points Redemption Reports

Points Redemption Reports in EquiBillBook track and analyze how customers redeem their reward points. These reports help monitor redemption patterns, assess redemption program effectiveness, and optimize redemption offerings to maximize customer engagement.

Understanding Points Redemption Reports

Points Redemption Reports help you:

  • Track points redemption activities
  • Monitor redemption patterns and trends
  • Assess redemption program performance
  • Identify popular redemption options
  • Optimize redemption offerings
  • Manage redemption costs

Accessing Points Redemption Reports

To generate points redemption reports:

  1. Navigate to the Reports module from the main menu
  2. Click on Reward Points Reports â†’ Redemption Reports or Points Redemption
  3. Select date range and filters
  4. Click "Generate Report"

    Troubleshooting

    Report shows no redemptions:

    • Check date range is correct
    • Verify redemptions exist in the period
    • Ensure filters are not too restrictive
    • Check redemption tracking is enabled

    Redemption totals don't match:

    • Verify all redemptions are recorded
    • Check date range selected
    • Review filters applied
    • Ensure calculations are correct

    Charts not displaying:

    • Ensure sufficient data exists
    • Try refreshing the report
    • Check browser compatibility
    • Verify JavaScript is enabled
