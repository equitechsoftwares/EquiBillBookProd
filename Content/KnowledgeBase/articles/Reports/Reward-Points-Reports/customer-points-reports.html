Customer Points Reports

Customer Points Reports in EquiBillBook provide detailed insights into individual customer reward points activities, balances, and redemption patterns. These reports help track customer engagement, manage customer relationships, and optimize reward points program effectiveness at the customer level.

Understanding Customer Points Reports

Customer Points Reports help you:

  • Track individual customer points activities
  • Monitor customer points balances
  • Analyze customer redemption patterns
  • Identify high-value customers
  • Manage customer relationships
  • Optimize customer engagement

Accessing Customer Points Reports

To generate customer points reports:

  1. Navigate to the Reports module from the main menu
  2. Click on Reward Points Reports â†’ Customer Points or Customer Points Report
  3. Select customer(s) and report parameters
  4. Click "Generate Report"

Customer Points Information

Points Balance

Current points information:

  • Current Balance: Available points balance
  • Pending Points: Points pending activation
  • Expiring Points: Points expiring soon
  • Expired Points: Points that expired
  • Points Value: Monetary value of points
  • Lifetime Points Earned: Total points ever earned

Points Earning History

Customer earning activities:

  • Total Points Earned: Lifetime earnings
  • Earning Transactions: Number of earning transactions
  • Average Points per Transaction: Average earning rate
  • Earning Sources: Breakdown by source
  • Earning Trends: Earning patterns over time
  • Recent Earnings: Latest earning activities

Points Redemption History

Customer redemption activities:

  • Total Points Redeemed: Lifetime redemptions
  • Redemption Transactions: Number of redemptions
  • Average Redemption Value: Average redemption amount
  • Redemption Types: Breakdown by type
  • Redemption Trends: Redemption patterns
  • Recent Redemptions: Latest redemption activities

Customer Engagement Metrics

Engagement indicators:

  • Account Status: Active/inactive status
  • Last Activity Date: Most recent activity
  • Activity Frequency: How often customer earns/redeems
  • Engagement Score: Overall engagement rating
  • Customer Tier: Points tier/level (if applicable)
  • Loyalty Status: Loyalty program status

Report Parameters

Customer Selection

Select customers to include:

  • All Customers: Include all customers
  • Specific Customer: Select one or more customers
  • Customer Group: Filter by customer category
  • Points Balance Range: Filter by balance range
  • Customer Status: Active/inactive customers
  • Engagement Level: High/medium/low engagement

Time Period Selection

Select the period for analysis:

  • All Time: Complete history
  • This Month: Current month
  • This Quarter: Current quarter
  • This Year: Current year
  • Last 30 Days: Last month
  • Last 90 Days: Last quarter
  • Custom Range: Select specific dates

Report Type Selection

Select report type:

  • Balance Report: Current balances
  • Earning Report: Earning activities
  • Redemption Report: Redemption activities
  • Activity Report: All activities
  • Summary Report: Comprehensive summary

Filter Options

Apply additional filters:

  • Location/Branch: Filter by location
  • Points Status: Active, expired, pending
  • Activity Type: Earning, redemption, expiration
  • Minimum Balance: Filter by minimum balance

    Troubleshooting

    Report shows no customer data:

    • Check customer selection is correct
    • Verify customer has points activity
    • Ensure filters are not too restrictive
    • Check date range is appropriate

    Points balance seems incorrect:

    • Verify all transactions are recorded
    • Check for pending points
    • Review expiration dates
    • Ensure calculations are correct

    Charts not displaying:

    • Ensure sufficient data exists
    • Try refreshing the report
    • Check browser compatibility
    • Verify JavaScript is enabled
