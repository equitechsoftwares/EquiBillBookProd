Tax Paid Reports

Tax Paid Reports provide detailed information about all taxes paid on purchase transactions, vendor bills, and expenses in EquiBillBook. This guide will help you generate and analyze tax paid reports effectively.

Understanding Tax Paid Reports

Tax Paid Reports help you:

  • Track all taxes paid on purchases
  • Monitor input tax credit (ITC) availability
  • Analyze tax payment trends
  • Prepare ITC claims and reconciliation
  • Ensure accurate tax payment tracking
  • Generate reports for tax compliance

Accessing Tax Paid Reports

To generate tax paid reports:

  1. Navigate to the Reports module from the main menu
  2. Click on Tax Reports â†’ Tax Paid Report
  3. Select date range and filters
  4. Click "Generate Report"

Report Information Displayed

Overall Tax Payment Summary

The summary shows:

  • Total Tax Paid: Sum of all taxes paid
  • Number of Taxable Transactions: Count of purchase transactions with tax
  • Average Tax per Transaction: Average tax amount
  • Tax Payment Rate: Percentage of purchases with tax
  • ITC Available: Input tax credit available for utilization
  • Tax by Type: Breakdown by tax types

Tax Payment Breakdown

Detailed breakdown includes:

  • Tax by Tax Type: CGST, SGST, IGST, UTGST, CESS, VAT, etc.
  • Tax by Tax Rate: 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%, etc.
  • Tax by Transaction Type: Purchase Bill, Purchase Order, Expense, etc.
  • Tax by Location: Tax payment by branch/location
  • Tax by Supplier: Tax payment by supplier/vendor
  • Tax by Item: Tax payment by item/product

Input Tax Credit (ITC) Details

ITC information includes:

  • ITC Available: Total ITC available for utilization
  • ITC Utilized: ITC already utilized
  • ITC Balance: Remaining ITC balance
  • ITC by Type: CGST, SGST, IGST ITC breakdown
  • ITC Eligibility: Eligible vs ineligible ITC

Tax Payment Details

Transaction-level details include:

  • Bill Number: Purchase bill reference
  • Bill Date: Date of transaction
  • Supplier Name: Supplier/vendor details
  • Taxable Value: Amount before tax
  • Tax Amount: Tax paid
  • Total Amount: Amount including tax
  • Tax Type: Type of tax applied
  • Tax Rate: Tax percentage
  • ITC Status: ITC available/claimed status

Report Parameters and Filters

Date Range Selection

Select the period for the report:

  • Predefined Ranges: Today, This Week, This Month, This Quarter, This Year
  • Custom Range: Select specific from and to dates
  • Last N Days: Last 7, 30, 60, 90 days
  • Period Comparison: Compare with previous period
  • Financial Year: Filter by financial year

Tax Type Filter

Filter by tax type:

  • All Tax Types: Include all tax types
  • Specific Tax Type: CGST, SGST, IGST, UTGST, CESS, VAT
  • Tax Group: Filter by tax group

ITC Status Filter

Filter by ITC status:

  • All: Include all transactions
  • ITC Available: Transactions with available ITC
  • ITC Utilized: Transactions with utilized ITC
  • ITC Not Eligible: Transactions not eligible for ITC

Location/Branch Filter

Filter by location or branch:

  • All Locations: Include all locations/branches
  • Specific Location: Select one or more locations
  • Location Group: Filter by location category

Supplier Filter

Filter by supplier:

  • All Suppliers: Include all suppliers
  • Specific Supplier: Select one or more suppliers
  • Supplier Group: Filter by supplier category

Transaction Type Filter

Filter by transaction type:

  • Purchase Bill: Regular purchase bills
  • Purchase Order: Purchase orders
  • Expense: Expense transactions
  • All: Include all transaction types

Report Format and Layout

Summary Section

The report typically includes:

  • Header: Report title, date range, company information
  • Summary Metrics: Key tax payment totals and ITC details
  • Breakdown Tables: Detailed breakdowns by tax type, rate, and category
  • ITC Summary: Input tax credit summary
  • Charts and Graphs: Visual representations (if available)
  • Footer: Report generation date, page numbers

Grouping Options

Group tax payment by:

  • Date (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • Tax Type
  • Tax Rate
  • Location/Branch
  • Supplier
  • Transaction Type
  • Item/Product
  • ITC Status

Generating and Viewing Reports

Generate Report

  1. Select date range
  2. Apply filters as needed
  3. Select grouping options
  4. Click "Generate Report" or "View Report"
  5. Review the generated report

Report Preview

The report preview shows:

  • Complete tax payment summary in preview format
  • Summary statistics at the top
  • ITC summary section
  • Detailed transaction breakdown
  • Visual charts and graphs
  • Options to export or print

    • Exporting Reports

    Export Formats

    Export reports in various formats:

    • Excel (XLS/XLSX): For analysis and manipulation
    • PDF: For printing and sharing
    • CSV: For data import to other systems

    Export Process

    1. Generate and preview the report
    2. Click "Export" or "Download"
    3. Select export format
    4. Choose export options (columns, grouping, etc.)
    5. Download the exported file

    Printing Reports

    To print reports:

    • Generate and preview the report
    • Click "Print" or "Print Preview"
    • Review print preview
    • Adjust print settings (orientation, margins, etc.)
    • Select pages to print if needed
    • Print or save as PDF

    Report Analysis

    Key Metrics to Review

    Analyze these metrics:

    • Total Tax Paid: Overall tax payment amount
    • Tax Payment Trends: Compare with previous periods
    • ITC Available: Total ITC available for utilization
    • ITC Utilization Rate: Percentage of ITC utilized
    • Tax by Type Distribution: Understand tax composition
    • Average Tax per Transaction: Track average tax amounts

    Trend Analysis

    Identify trends:

    • Compare periods to see tax payment trends
    • Identify seasonal tax patterns
    • Track ITC availability over time
    • Monitor ITC utilization trends

    ITC Management

    Use tax paid reports for ITC management:

    • Track ITC Availability: Monitor available ITC
    • Plan ITC Utilization: Plan ITC set-off against tax liability
    • Verify ITC Eligibility: Ensure ITC claims are valid
    • Reconcile ITC: Match ITC with tax paid
    • Monitor ITC Balance: Track remaining ITC balance

    Tax Payment Reconciliation

    Reconcile tax paid:

    • Match tax paid with purchase bills
    • Verify tax calculations are correct
    • Check for missing tax entries
    • Ensure all taxable purchases have tax
    • Validate tax rates applied correctly
    • Verify supplier GST details

    Best Practices

    • Generate reports regularly (daily, weekly, monthly)
    • Use consistent date ranges for comparison
    • Export reports for tax filing purposes
    • Review reports for accuracy and completeness
    • Monitor ITC availability regularly
    • Use filters to focus on specific tax types
    • Compare with previous periods to track trends
    • Reconcile tax paid with purchases regularly
    • Keep exported reports for audit purposes

    Troubleshooting

    Report shows no data:

    • Check date range is correct
    • Verify filters are not too restrictive
    • Ensure purchase transactions with tax exist for the selected period
    • Check location/branch selection

    Tax amounts don't match expected:

    • Verify all purchase bills are recorded
    • Check tax rates are configured correctly
    • Review tax calculations on bills
    • Verify tax groups are assigned correctly

    ITC not showing correctly:

    • Check purchase bills are recorded with tax
    • Verify supplier GST details are correct
    • Review ITC eligibility rules
    • Check reverse charge transactions
