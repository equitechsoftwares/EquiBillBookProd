KOT Summary Reports

KOT Summary Reports provide a comprehensive overview of all Kitchen Order Tickets (KOTs) in EquiBillBook. These reports help you understand overall kitchen performance, order volumes, revenue, and operational efficiency at a glance.

What are KOT Summary Reports?

KOT Summary Reports consolidate key metrics from all KOTs over a selected period, providing:

  • Total KOTs count and volume
  • Revenue summary from KOTs
  • Average order value
  • KOT status distribution
  • Order trends and patterns
  • Performance indicators

Accessing KOT Summary Reports

To generate KOT Summary Reports:

  1. Navigate to Reports → KOT Reports → KOT Summary
  2. Select the date range for analysis
  3. Configure filters (if needed)
  4. Click "Generate Report"

Report Parameters

Configure the following parameters:

  • Date Range: Select period (Today, This Week, This Month, This Year, Custom Range)
  • Business Location: Filter by branch/location (if multi-branch)
  • KOT Status: Filter by status (All, Pending, In Progress, Completed, Cancelled)
  • Table: Filter by specific table (optional)
  • Floor: Filter by restaurant floor (optional)
  • Station: Filter by kitchen station (optional)

Report Contents

KOT Summary Reports typically include:

  • Total KOTs: Count of all KOTs in the period
  • Total Revenue: Sum of all KOT amounts
  • Average Order Value: Average amount per KOT
  • Status Breakdown: Distribution of KOTs by status
  • Standalone vs Linked: Comparison of standalone KOTs vs booking-linked KOTs
  • Daily Trends: Day-by-day order volume and revenue
  • Peak Hours: Identification of busiest hours
  • Completion Rate: Percentage of completed vs total KOTs
  • Average Preparation Time: Average time to complete orders

Understanding the Report

Key metrics to analyze:

  • Order Volume: Track total number of orders to understand business activity
  • Revenue Performance: Monitor total revenue from KOTs
  • Order Efficiency: Review completion rates and preparation times
  • Status Distribution: Understand workflow efficiency through status breakdown
  • Trends: Identify patterns in order volumes and revenue
  • Peak Periods: Identify busy times for staffing and capacity planning
