Item-wise KOT Reports

Item-wise KOT Reports provide detailed analysis of Kitchen Order Tickets (KOTs) broken down by individual menu items in EquiBillBook. These reports help you understand which items are popular, identify best-sellers, track item performance, and make informed menu and inventory decisions.

What are Item-wise KOT Reports?

Item-wise KOT Reports analyze KOT data at the individual item level, providing:

  • Quantity sold per item
  • Revenue per item
  • Item popularity rankings
  • Performance trends by item
  • Slow-moving items identification
  • Item-level profitability analysis

Accessing Item-wise KOT Reports

To generate Item-wise KOT Reports:

  1. Navigate to Reports → KOT Reports → Item-wise KOT Reports
  2. Select the date range for analysis
  3. Configure filters (item, category, station, etc.)
  4. Click "Generate Report"

Report Parameters

Configure the following parameters:

  • Date Range: Select period (Today, This Week, This Month, This Year, Custom Range)
  • Business Location: Filter by branch/location (if multi-branch)
  • Item: Filter by specific item (optional)
  • Category: Filter by item category (optional)
  • Station: Filter by kitchen station (optional)
  • KOT Status: Filter by KOT status (optional)
  • Sort By: Sort by quantity, revenue, or name

Report Contents

Item-wise KOT Reports typically include:

  • Item Name: Name of the menu item
  • Item Code: Item code/SKU
  • Category: Item category
  • Quantity Sold: Total quantity ordered
  • Total Revenue: Total revenue from the item
  • Average Price: Average selling price
  • Number of Orders: Count of KOTs containing this item
  • Percentage of Total: Item's contribution to total revenue
  • Ranking: Popularity ranking
  • Trend: Performance trend (increasing/decreasing)

Understanding the Report

Key insights to analyze:

  • Best Sellers: Identify top-performing items by quantity and revenue
  • Slow Movers: Identify items with low sales volumes
  • Revenue Contribution: Understand which items contribute most to revenue
  • Menu Performance: Evaluate overall menu effectiveness
  • Inventory Planning: Plan inventory based on item popularity
  • Pricing Strategy: Analyze pricing effectiveness
  • Trend Analysis: Identify items with increasing or decreasing popularity
