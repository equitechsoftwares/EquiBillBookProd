KOT Timing Reports

KOT Timing Reports provide detailed analysis of time-related metrics for Kitchen Order Tickets (KOTs) in EquiBillBook. These reports help you understand preparation times, identify delays, optimize kitchen efficiency, and improve customer service by analyzing timing patterns.

What are KOT Timing Reports?

KOT Timing Reports analyze time-based metrics for KOTs, providing:

  • Average preparation times
  • Time by item or category
  • Time by station
  • Peak time analysis
  • Delay identification
  • Efficiency trends

Accessing KOT Timing Reports

To generate KOT Timing Reports:

  1. Navigate to Reports → KOT Reports → KOT Timing Reports
  2. Select the date range for analysis
  3. Configure filters (item, station, time range, etc.)
  4. Click "Generate Report"

Report Parameters

Configure the following parameters:

  • Date Range: Select period (Today, This Week, This Month, This Year, Custom Range)
  • Business Location: Filter by branch/location (if multi-branch)
  • Time Range: Filter by specific hours (optional)
  • Item: Filter by specific item (optional)
  • Category: Filter by item category (optional)
  • Station: Filter by kitchen station (optional)
  • KOT Status: Filter by KOT status (optional)

Report Contents

KOT Timing Reports typically include:

  • Average Preparation Time: Overall average time to complete orders
  • Time by Item: Average preparation time for each menu item
  • Time by Category: Average time by item category
  • Time by Station: Average time by kitchen station
  • Peak Time Analysis: Preparation times during peak hours
  • Fastest Items: Items with shortest preparation times
  • Slowest Items: Items with longest preparation times
  • Time Trends: Preparation time trends over the period
  • Delay Analysis: Orders exceeding expected time
  • Efficiency Metrics: Time-based efficiency indicators

Understanding the Report
