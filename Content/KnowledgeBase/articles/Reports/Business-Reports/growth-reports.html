Growth Reports

Growth Reports in EquiBillBook track and analyze business growth trends over time. These reports help you understand growth patterns, identify growth drivers, measure growth rates, and make strategic decisions to sustain and accelerate business growth.

Understanding Growth Reports

Growth Reports help you:

  • Track business growth over time
  • Measure growth rates and trends
  • Identify growth drivers and opportunities
  • Compare growth across different dimensions
  • Forecast future growth
  • Make strategic growth decisions

Accessing Growth Reports

To generate growth reports:

  1. Navigate to the Reports module from the main menu
  2. Click on Business Reports â†’ Growth Reports or Growth Analysis
  3. Select growth metrics and time period
  4. Click "Generate Report"

Types of Growth Metrics

Revenue Growth

Track revenue growth:

  • Total Revenue Growth: Overall revenue growth rate
  • Sales Growth: Sales revenue growth
  • Monthly Growth Rate: Month-over-month growth
  • Year-over-Year Growth: Annual growth rate
  • Compound Growth Rate: CAGR calculation
  • Revenue Growth Trend: Growth trend over time

Customer Growth

Track customer base growth:

  • Customer Count Growth: Number of customers growth
  • New Customer Acquisition: New customers added
  • Customer Retention Rate: Customer retention
  • Customer Lifetime Value Growth: CLV growth
  • Active Customer Growth: Active customers growth

Product Growth

Track product performance growth:

  • Product Sales Growth: Product revenue growth
  • Category Growth: Category-wise growth
  • Top Product Growth: Best-selling products growth
  • New Product Performance: New product growth
  • Product Mix Growth: Product portfolio growth

Profitability Growth

Track profitability growth:

  • Profit Growth: Net profit growth
  • Profit Margin Growth: Margin improvement
  • Gross Profit Growth: Gross profit growth
  • Operating Profit Growth: Operating profit growth
  • Profitability Trend: Profitability over time

Operational Growth

Track operational metrics growth:

  • Transaction Volume Growth: Transaction count growth
  • Average Transaction Value Growth: AOV growth
  • Inventory Turnover Growth: Stock turnover improvement
  • Efficiency Growth: Operational efficiency improvement

Generating Reports

Generate Growth Report

  1. Select time period
  2. Choose growth metrics
  3. Select comparison period
  4. Apply filters if needed
  5. Click "Generate Report"
  6. Review growth analysis

    Troubleshooting

    Growth rates seem incorrect:

    • Verify date ranges are correct
    • Check data completeness for both periods
    • Ensure filters are consistent
    • Review calculation methods

    No growth data available:

    • Check date range has sufficient data
    • Verify transactions exist for the period
    • Ensure filters are not too restrictive
    • Check location/branch selection

    Charts not displaying:

    • Ensure sufficient data exists
    • Try refreshing the report
    • Check browser compatibility
    • Verify JavaScript is enabled
