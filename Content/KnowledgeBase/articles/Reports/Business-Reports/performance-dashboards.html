Performance Dashboards

Performance Dashboards in EquiBillBook provide real-time visual representations of your business performance metrics. These interactive dashboards help you monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), track business trends, and make data-driven decisions quickly.

Understanding Performance Dashboards

Performance Dashboards help you:

  • Monitor real-time business performance
  • Track key performance indicators (KPIs)
  • Visualize business metrics with charts and graphs
  • Identify trends and patterns quickly
  • Make informed business decisions
  • Monitor business health at a glance

Accessing Performance Dashboards

To access performance dashboards:

  1. Navigate to the Reports module from the main menu
  2. Click on Business Reports â†’ Performance Dashboard or Dashboard
  3. The dashboard will load with default settings
  4. Customize the view as needed

Dashboard Components

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

KPIs displayed on the dashboard include:

  • Total Revenue: Current period revenue
  • Total Expenses: Current period expenses
  • Net Profit: Revenue minus expenses
  • Profit Margin: Profit percentage
  • Cash Balance: Current cash position
  • Accounts Receivable: Outstanding receivables
  • Accounts Payable: Outstanding payables
  • Sales Growth: Percentage growth
  • Customer Count: Active customers
  • Inventory Value: Current stock value

Revenue Metrics

Revenue-related widgets include:

  • Sales Trend Chart: Revenue over time
  • Sales by Category: Revenue breakdown
  • Top Customers: Highest revenue customers
  • Top Products: Best-selling items
  • Sales by Location: Branch-wise sales
  • Payment Method Distribution: Payment type breakdown

Expense Metrics

Expense-related widgets include:

  • Expense Trend Chart: Expenses over time
  • Expense by Category: Category breakdown
  • Top Expense Categories: Highest expense areas
  • Expense vs Budget: Budget comparison
  • Purchase Trends: Purchase patterns

Inventory Metrics

Inventory-related widgets include:

  • Stock Value: Current inventory value
  • Low Stock Alerts: Items below reorder level
  • Stock Turnover: Inventory turnover rate
  • Fast-moving Items: High sales velocity
  • Slow-moving Items: Low sales velocity

Financial Health Metrics

Financial health indicators include:

  • Cash Flow Chart: Cash inflows and outflows
  • Profitability Trend: Profit over time
  • Debt-to-Equity Ratio: Financial leverage
  • Current Ratio: Liquidity indicator
  • Working Capital: Current assets minus liabilities

Customize which widgets to display:

  • This Week: Current week
  • This Month: Current month

Filter Options

Apply filters to dashboard data:

  • Category: Filter by category
  • User: Filter by user/employee

Export Options

Troubleshooting

Dashboard not loading:

  • Check internet connection
  • Refresh the page
  • Clear browser cache
  • Check browser compatibility
  • Verify JavaScript is enabled

Data not updating:

  • Check date range selection
  • Verify filters are not too restrictive
  • Refresh the dashboard manually
  • Check for data entry delays
  • Verify user permissions

Charts not displaying:

  • Ensure sufficient data exists
  • Check date range has data
  • Verify filters allow data display
  • Try refreshing the dashboard
  • Check browser console for errors
