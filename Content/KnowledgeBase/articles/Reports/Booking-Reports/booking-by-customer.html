Booking by Customer

Booking by Customer reports provide detailed analysis of customer booking patterns, helping you identify loyal customers, understand customer preferences, and improve customer relationship management.

What are Booking by Customer Reports?

Booking by Customer reports provide:

  • Complete booking history for each customer
  • Booking frequency and patterns
  • Customer preferences and behavior
  • Total spending per customer
  • Customer loyalty metrics
  • VIP customer identification

Why Booking by Customer Reports are Important

These reports help you:

  • Identify loyal and repeat customers
  • Understand customer booking patterns
  • Personalize customer service
  • Plan customer retention strategies
  • Identify VIP customers
  • Analyze customer preferences
  • Improve customer relationships
  • Maximize customer lifetime value

Accessing Booking by Customer Reports

To view Booking by Customer reports:

  1. Navigate to Reports â†’ Booking Reports
  2. Select Booking by Customer or Customer Booking History
  3. Choose specific customer(s) or view all
  4. Set the date range for analysis
  5. Apply filters if needed
  6. Generate the report

Booking by Customer Report Information

The report typically displays for each customer:

  • Customer Name: Customer details and contact information
  • Total Bookings: Number of bookings made
  • Booking Frequency: How often customer books
  • First Booking Date: Date of first reservation
  • Last Booking Date: Date of most recent reservation
  • Total Revenue: Total spending from bookings
  • Average Revenue per Booking: Average spending per visit
  • Preferred Tables: Most frequently booked tables
  • Preferred Times: Most common booking times
  • Preferred Days: Most common booking days
  • Booking Status: Confirmed, cancelled, no-show breakdown

Filtering Booking by Customer Reports

You can filter reports by:

  • Customer: Specific customer or multiple customers
  • Date Range: Daily, weekly, monthly, or custom periods
  • Booking Status: Confirmed, cancelled, completed, no-show
  • Minimum Bookings: Filter by booking frequency
  • Revenue Range: Filter by total spending
  • Customer Category: Filter by customer categories

Customer Booking History

For each customer, view detailed booking history:

  • Booking Date: Date of each reservation
  • Booking Time: Time of reservation
  • Table: Table assigned or requested
  • Party Size: Number of guests
  • Status: Booking status
  • Revenue: Revenue from the booking
  • Special Requests: Any special requirements
  • Waiter: Assigned waiter

Customer Loyalty Metrics

Key metrics for customer analysis:

  • Booking Frequency: Number of bookings per period
  • Visit Recency: Time since last booking
  • Customer Lifetime Value: Total revenue from customer
  • Retention Rate: Likelihood of repeat bookings
  • Average Booking Value: Average spending per visit
  • Booking Consistency: Regularity of bookings

VIP Customer Identification

Identify VIP customers based on:

  • High booking frequency
  • High total spending
  • Consistent booking patterns
  • Long-term customer relationship
  • Low cancellation rate
  • Positive booking history

Customer Preferences Analysis

Understand customer preferences:

  • Preferred Tables: Tables most frequently booked
  • Preferred Times: Most common booking times
  • Preferred Days: Most common booking days
  • Party Size Patterns: Typical group size
  • Special Requests: Common special requirements
  • Seasonal Patterns: Booking patterns by season

Customer Segmentation

Segment customers by:

  • High-Value Customers: Top spenders
  • Frequent Customers: Regular visitors
  • Occasional Customers: Infrequent visitors
  • New Customers: Recent first-time bookings
  • At-Risk Customers: Declining booking frequency
