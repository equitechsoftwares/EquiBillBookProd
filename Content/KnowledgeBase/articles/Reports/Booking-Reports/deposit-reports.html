Deposit Reports

Deposit Reports provide comprehensive analysis of booking deposits, helping you track deposit collection, refunds, outstanding deposits, and the financial impact of deposit management on your restaurant operations.

What are Deposit Reports?

Deposit Reports provide:

  • Total deposits collected
  • Deposit refunds issued
  • Outstanding deposits
  • Deposit trends over time
  • Deposit by booking status
  • Financial impact analysis

Why Deposit Reports are Important

These reports help you:

  • Track deposit collection and management
  • Monitor deposit refunds
  • Identify outstanding deposits
  • Analyze deposit trends
  • Evaluate deposit policy effectiveness
  • Manage cash flow related to deposits
  • Reconcile deposit accounts
  • Support financial reporting

Accessing Deposit Reports

To view Deposit Reports:

  1. Navigate to Reports â†’ Booking Reports
  2. Select Deposit Reports
  3. Set the date range for analysis
  4. Apply filters if needed (booking status, customer)
  5. Generate the report

Deposit Report Information

The report typically displays:

  • Total Deposits Collected: Grand total of all deposits
  • Total Deposits Refunded: Total refunds issued
  • Outstanding Deposits: Deposits not yet refunded
  • Deposits Retained: Non-refundable deposits
  • Number of Deposits: Count of deposit transactions
  • Average Deposit Amount: Average deposit per booking
  • Deposit Collection Rate: Percentage of bookings with deposits
  • Deposit Refund Rate: Percentage of deposits refunded

Filtering Deposit Reports

You can filter reports by:

  • Date Range: Daily, weekly, monthly, or custom periods
  • Booking Status: Confirmed, cancelled, completed, no-show
  • Customer: Filter by specific customers
  • Deposit Status: Collected, refunded, outstanding, retained
  • Location/Floor: Filter by restaurant floor
  • Amount Range: Filter by deposit amount range

Deposit Collection Analysis

Analysis of deposits collected:

  • Total Collected: Sum of all deposits received
  • Collection by Period: Deposits by time period
  • Collection Trends: Trends over time
  • Average Deposit: Average amount per deposit
  • Deposit Methods: Payment methods used for deposits
  • Collection Rate: Percentage of bookings requiring deposits

Deposit Refund Analysis

Analysis of deposit refunds:

  • Total Refunded: Sum of all refunds issued
  • Refund by Reason: Reasons for refunds
  • Refund Trends: Refund patterns over time
  • Refund Processing Time: Time to process refunds
  • Refund Methods: Methods used for refunds
  • Refund Rate: Percentage of deposits refunded

Outstanding Deposits

Deposits not yet refunded:

  • Total Outstanding: Deposits awaiting refund
  • Outstanding by Status: Breakdown by booking status
  • Outstanding Duration: How long deposits have been outstanding
  • Pending Refunds: Refunds in process
  • Eligible for Refund: Deposits that can be refunded

Deposits Retained

Non-refundable deposits:

  • Total Retained: Deposits kept due to cancellations/no-shows
  • Retention by Reason: Reasons for retention
  • Retention Rate: Percentage of deposits retained
  • Revenue Protection: Financial protection from deposits
