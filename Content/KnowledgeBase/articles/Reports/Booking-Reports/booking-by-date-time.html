Booking by Date/Time

Booking by Date/Time reports provide detailed analysis of bookings organized by specific dates and times, helping you identify peak periods, plan capacity, and optimize operations based on temporal patterns.

What are Booking by Date/Time Reports?

Booking by Date/Time reports provide:

  • Bookings organized by specific dates
  • Bookings organized by time slots
  • Peak booking periods identification
  • Time-based booking patterns
  • Date-wise booking trends
  • Capacity planning insights

Why Booking by Date/Time Reports are Important

These reports help you:

  • Identify peak booking periods
  • Plan capacity and staffing
  • Optimize table availability
  • Understand booking patterns
  • Make data-driven scheduling decisions
  • Identify slow periods for promotions
  • Maximize revenue during peak times
  • Improve operational efficiency

Accessing Booking by Date/Time Reports

To view Booking by Date/Time reports:

  1. Navigate to Reports â†’ Booking Reports
  2. Select Booking by Date/Time or Time-based Booking Reports
  3. Set the date range for analysis
  4. Choose time granularity (hourly, daily, weekly, monthly)
  5. Apply filters if needed
  6. Generate the report

Booking by Date Report Information

The report typically displays for each date:

  • Date: Specific date
  • Day of Week: Monday, Tuesday, etc.
  • Total Bookings: Number of bookings for the date
  • Confirmed Bookings: Number of confirmed reservations
  • Cancelled Bookings: Number of cancelled reservations
  • Total Revenue: Revenue generated on the date
  • Average Party Size: Average guests per booking
  • Peak Hours: Busiest time periods
  • Table Utilization: Percentage of tables booked

Booking by Time Report Information

The report typically displays for each time slot:

  • Time Slot: Specific hour or time range
  • Total Bookings: Number of bookings in the time slot
  • Available Tables: Tables available during the time
  • Booked Tables: Tables booked during the time
  • Utilization Rate: Percentage of tables booked
  • Total Revenue: Revenue generated in the time slot
  • Average Booking Duration: Average length of bookings
  • Peak Status: Whether it's a peak period

Filtering Booking by Date/Time Reports

You can filter reports by:

  • Date Range: Specific dates or date ranges
  • Time Range: Specific time periods
  • Day of Week: Filter by specific days
  • Table: Filter by specific tables
  • Booking Status: Confirmed, cancelled, completed
  • Location/Floor: Filter by restaurant floor
  • Table Type: Filter by table types
