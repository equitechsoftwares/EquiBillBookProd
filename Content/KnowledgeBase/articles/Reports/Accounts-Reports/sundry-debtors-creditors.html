Sundry Debtors/Creditors

The Sundry Debtors/Creditors report in EquiBillBook provides a comprehensive view of all outstanding amounts owed to your business (debtors) and amounts your business owes to others (creditors). It helps you track receivables and payables, manage collections and payments, and maintain accurate accounts receivable and accounts payable records. This guide explains how to generate and understand the Sundry Debtors/Creditors report.

What is Sundry Debtors/Creditors Report?

The Sundry Debtors/Creditors report shows:

  • Sundry Debtors: Amounts owed to your business by customers and others
  • Sundry Creditors: Amounts your business owes to suppliers and others
  • Individual debtor/creditor balances
  • Total outstanding receivables
  • Total outstanding payables
  • Aging analysis of outstanding amounts
  • Transaction details for debtors/creditors

Purpose of Sundry Debtors/Creditors Report

The Sundry Debtors/Creditors report is used to:

  • Track accounts receivable (debtors)
  • Track accounts payable (creditors)
  • Monitor outstanding balances
  • Manage collections from debtors
  • Plan payments to creditors
  • Analyze aging of receivables and payables
  • Identify overdue amounts
  • Support cash flow management

Accessing Sundry Debtors/Creditors Report

To generate a Sundry Debtors/Creditors report:

  1. Navigate to Reports from the main menu
  2. Go to Accounts Reports
  3. Select Sundry Debtors/Creditors or Debtors & Creditors
  4. Select report type (Debtors, Creditors, or Both)
  5. Set the As of Date (the date for which you want the report)
  6. Select branch (if using multi-branch setup)
  7. Configure additional filters if needed
  8. Click "Generate" or "View Report"

Understanding Sundry Debtors/Creditors Structure

1. Sundry Debtors Section

Shows amounts owed to your business:

  • Customer Balances: Outstanding amounts from customers
  • Other Debtors: Other parties who owe money to your business
  • Individual Balances: Balance for each debtor
  • Total Debtors: Sum of all debtor balances
  • Aging Analysis: Breakdown by age of outstanding amounts

2. Sundry Creditors Section

Shows amounts your business owes:

  • Supplier Balances: Outstanding amounts to suppliers
  • Other Creditors: Other parties to whom your business owes money
  • Individual Balances: Balance for each creditor
  • Total Creditors: Sum of all creditor balances
  • Aging Analysis: Breakdown by age of outstanding amounts

3. Aging Analysis

Shows outstanding amounts by age:

  • Current: Not yet due
  • 0-30 Days: Due within 30 days
  • 31-60 Days: 31 to 60 days overdue
  • 61-90 Days: 61 to 90 days overdue
  • Over 90 Days: More than 90 days overdue
  • Total Outstanding: Sum of all aging categories

Report Configuration Options

1. Report Type Selection

Choose what to include:

  • Sundry Debtors Only: Show only receivables
  • Sundry Creditors Only: Show only payables
  • Both Debtors and Creditors: Show both sections

2. Date Selection

Choose the date for the report:

  • Today: Current date's outstanding balances
  • End of Month: Month-end balances
  • End of Quarter: Quarter-end balances
  • End of Financial Year: Year-end balances
  • Specific Date: Any date you want to review

3. Filter Options

Filter the report:

  • Show Zero Balances: Include parties with zero balance
  • Hide Zero Balances: Exclude parties with zero balance
  • Show Only Overdue: Show only overdue amounts
  • Filter by Customer/Supplier: Select specific parties
  • Filter by Amount Range: Show balances within range

4. Display Options

Customize report display:

  • Show Aging Analysis: Display aging breakdown
  • Show Transaction Details: Include transaction list
  • Group by Customer/Supplier: Organize by party
  • Show Contact Information: Include contact details
  • Show Payment Terms: Display payment terms
