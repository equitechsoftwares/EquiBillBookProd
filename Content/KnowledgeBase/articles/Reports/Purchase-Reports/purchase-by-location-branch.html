Purchase by Location/Branch

Purchase by Location/Branch Reports provide detailed insights into purchasing activities across different business locations, helping you analyze location-specific patterns, allocate budgets, and optimize multi-location procurement. This guide explains how to use Purchase by Location/Branch Reports in EquiBillBook.

What are Purchase by Location/Branch Reports?

These reports show:

  • Total purchases per location/branch
  • Purchase patterns by location
  • Location-wise spending analysis
  • Comparison across locations
  • Location-specific supplier relationships
  • Item purchases by location
  • Budget allocation by location

Why Purchase by Location/Branch Reports are Important

These reports help you:

  • Track purchasing across multiple locations
  • Allocate budgets by location
  • Compare purchasing patterns
  • Identify location-specific needs
  • Optimize centralized vs. decentralized purchasing
  • Manage multi-location inventory
  • Control location-wise spending

Accessing Purchase by Location/Branch Reports

To view Purchase by Location/Branch Reports:

  1. Navigate to Reports â†’ Purchase Reports
  2. Select Purchase by Location or Purchase by Branch
  3. Set the date range for analysis
  4. Select specific locations or view all
  5. Apply additional filters if needed
  6. Generate the report

Purchase by Location Report Information

The report typically displays:

  • Location/Branch Name: Location identifier
  • Total Purchase Value: Total purchases per location
  • Number of Transactions: Purchase count per location
  • Average Purchase Value: Average per transaction
  • Total Quantity: Units purchased per location
  • Total Paid: Amount paid per location
  • Total Due: Outstanding per location
  • Purchase by Category: Category breakdown per location
  • Purchase by Supplier: Supplier breakdown per location
  • Purchase Trends: Trends per location

Filtering Purchase by Location Reports

You can filter reports by:

  • Date Range: Analyze purchases for specific periods
  • Location/Branch: Filter by specific locations
  • Supplier: Filter by suppliers
  • Item Category: Filter by categories
  • Item: Filter by specific items
  • Payment Status: Paid, unpaid, or all
  • Document Type: Bills, Orders, Quotations
