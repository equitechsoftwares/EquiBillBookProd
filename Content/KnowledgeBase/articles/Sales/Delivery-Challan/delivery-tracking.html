Delivery Tracking

Delivery tracking in EquiBillBook helps you monitor the status and location of goods throughout the delivery process. This feature ensures transparency, improves customer communication, and helps identify and resolve delivery issues promptly.

Understanding Delivery Tracking

Delivery tracking allows you to:

  • Monitor the real-time status of deliveries
  • Track shipment progress from dispatch to delivery
  • Provide customers with delivery updates
  • Identify delays or issues in the delivery process
  • Maintain accurate delivery records

Setting Up Delivery Tracking

Adding Tracking Information

When creating or updating a delivery challan:

  1. Enter Tracking Number provided by the shipping carrier
  2. Select or enter the Shipping Carrier name
  3. Add Shipping Method (Standard, Express, Overnight, etc.)
  4. Enter Dispatch Date when goods are sent
  5. Add Expected Delivery Date if available

Shipping Details

Include comprehensive shipping information:

  • Courier/Shipping Company: Name of the shipping service
  • Tracking URL: Link to carrier's tracking page
  • Waybill Number: Shipping document reference
  • Shipping Address: Complete delivery address
  • Contact Person: Recipient name and phone number

Tracking Delivery Status

Status Updates

Update delivery status as goods move through the process:

  • Pending: Challan created, awaiting dispatch
  • Dispatched: Goods have left your premises
  • In Transit: Goods are being transported
  • Out for Delivery: Goods are with local delivery agent
  • Delivered: Successfully delivered to customer
  • Failed Delivery: Delivery attempt unsuccessful
  • Returned: Goods returned to sender

Updating Status Manually

To update delivery status:

  1. Open the delivery challan details
  2. Go to the tracking or status section
  3. Select the new status from the dropdown
  4. Add status update notes if needed
  5. Save the changes

Tracking Information Fields

Basic Tracking

  • Tracking Number: Unique identifier from shipping carrier
  • Carrier: Shipping company name
  • Status: Current delivery status
  • Last Updated: Date and time of last status update

Advanced Tracking

  • Origin Location: Where shipment started
  • Current Location: Latest known location
  • Destination: Final delivery address
  • Estimated Arrival: Expected delivery date/time
  • Actual Delivery: Confirmed delivery date/time

Tracking History

Maintain a complete tracking history:

  • Track delivery attempts and outcomes
  • Maintain audit trail for compliance

Handling Delivery Issues

Failed Deliveries

When delivery fails:

  • Update status to "Failed Delivery"
  • Record reason for failure
  • Schedule re-delivery if needed
  • Notify customer about the issue
  • Update tracking information

Delayed Deliveries

For delayed shipments:

  • Identify the delay reason
  • Update expected delivery date
  • Communicate with customer
  • Track new delivery timeline
  • Document delay for analysis

Returned Shipments

When goods are returned:

  • Update status to "Returned"
  • Record return reason
  • Update inventory if applicable
  • Process refund or re-delivery
  • Maintain return records

Best Practices for Delivery Tracking

  • Always enter tracking numbers when available
  • Update delivery status promptly as it changes
  • Maintain accurate and complete tracking information
  • Communicate proactively with customers about delivery status
  • Document all tracking milestones and issues
  • Review tracking reports regularly to identify patterns
  • Use consistent status naming across all deliveries
  • Set up automated tracking updates if possible
  • Train staff on proper tracking procedures
  • Monitor delivery performance metrics

Troubleshooting

Tracking number is not working?

  • Verify tracking number is entered correctly
  • Check if carrier supports online tracking
  • Confirm tracking number format matches carrier requirements
  • Contact carrier for tracking assistance

Status updates are not reflecting?

  • Refresh the challan details page
  • Check if status was saved correctly
  • Verify you have permission to update status
  • Clear browser cache and try again

Can't find tracking information?

  • Check the challan details page
  • Look in the shipping/tracking section
  • Review challan history for tracking updates
  • Contact the shipping carrier directly
