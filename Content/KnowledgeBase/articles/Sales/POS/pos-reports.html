POS Reports

POS reports provide valuable insights into your sales performance, inventory movement, cash operations, and business trends. This guide covers all available POS reports and how to use them effectively.

What are POS Reports?

POS reports are analytical summaries of your Point of Sale transactions, providing detailed information about sales, payments, products, customers, and cash operations. These reports help you make informed business decisions and track performance.

Accessing POS Reports

To view POS reports:

  1. Navigate to Reports module from main menu
  2. Go to POS Reports or Sales Reports
  3. Select the type of report you want
  4. Set date range and filters
  5. Generate the report

Sales Reports

POS Sales Summary Report

Overview of all POS sales:

  • Total Sales: Grand total of all POS sales
  • Number of Transactions: Count of sales
  • Average Transaction Value: Average sale amount
  • Sales by Payment Method: Cash, Card, UPI breakdown
  • Sales by Date: Daily sales summary
  • Comparison: Compare with previous periods

POS Sales Detail Report

Detailed transaction listing:

  • Individual transaction details
  • Invoice numbers and dates
  • Customer information
  • Items sold in each transaction
  • Payment methods used
  • Tax and discount details

Hourly Sales Report

Sales performance by hour:

  • Sales volume by time of day
  • Peak hours identification
  • Transaction count by hour
  • Helps with staffing decisions

Using Reports for Business Decisions

Staffing Decisions

Use reports to:

  • Schedule staff based on peak hours

  • Recognize top performers

Pricing Strategy

Use reports to:

  • Analyze discount impact
  • Adjust pricing based on sales data
  • Identify pricing opportunities

Best Practices
